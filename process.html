02 · process

How we work together.

Four clear stages. Honest timelines. No surprise invoices at the end.

01

Discovery call

›
A 30-minute free call. I ask where it hurts, you tell me what you've tried. No sales pitch, no deck. If I'm not the right fit, I'll say so and point you somewhere that is. // 30 min · free
02

Tenant audit

›
Read-only access to your tenant. I run the full audit covering security posture, identity config, license utilization, and mail flow, and send back a short, plain-English findings doc. // 3 to 5 days · fixed fee
03

Scoped plan

›
You get a prioritised plan: what to fix now, what can wait, and what's nice-to-have. Every item has an effort estimate and a fixed price. You pick what moves forward. // 1 to 2 days
04

Execution

›
I do the work. You get a weekly status note, a live change log, and documentation checked into your own repo or SharePoint. Nothing lives in my head. // 1 to 3 weeks, scope-dependent
